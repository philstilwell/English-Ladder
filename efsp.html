Healthcare Administration English

A challenging professional English curriculum for healthcare administration teams who need precise language for access, patient flow, revenue cycle, quality, compliance, staffing, service recovery, care coordination, and executive reporting.

Core topics

  • Patient Access, Scheduling, and Referrals: Turn access problems into measurable workflow and patient-impact questions.
  • Revenue Cycle, Coding, and Denials: Discuss payment operations without blaming front-desk, clinical, or billing teams.
  • Patient Flow, Capacity, and Staffing: Explain bottlenecks using census, throughput, acuity, and staffing language.
  • Quality, Safety, and Accreditation: Use safety language that is factual, nonpunitive, and escalation-ready.
  • HIPAA, Privacy, and Information Governance: Set privacy boundaries without sounding obstructive.
  • Patient Experience and Service Recovery: Handle complaints with empathy, facts, and process accountability.
  • Population Health and Care Coordination: Explain care gaps, readmission risk, and coordination without overpromising outcomes.
  • Executive Dashboards and Board Updates: Translate operational data into concise executive risk and decision language.

Materials included

  • Instructor guide with eight 90-minute module plans
  • Participant workbook with customized field-specific practice pages
  • Dialogue lab with realistic workplace conversations and observer checklists
  • Jargon quick reference with terminology, contrast pairs, and pressure-tested sentence frames

Cultural Leadership in US Branches

The course is built for advanced Japanese and Chinese managers who need more than vocabulary. It gives learners a map of American workplace directness and repeated practice with the line between confident leadership and sufficient pushback.

Core topics

  • Reading American idea-combat without assuming hostility
  • Using evidence-based pushback without over-apologizing
  • Managing interruptions, public disagreement, and meeting closure
  • Giving direct feedback while preserving dignity
  • Translating between headquarters expectations and US branch norms

Materials included

  • Instructor guide with eight 90-minute module plans
  • Participant workbook with practice pages and reflection prompts
  • Scenario cards for role-play and coaching
  • Quick reference for in-the-moment pushback language
